My name is Emily, and I am a Psychology student that's done watercolour painting as a hobby for 5 years now.

Materials used

  • A5 canvases
  • Pallette
  • Paintbrushes

  • Ever since I've learned some HTML and CSS, I've wanted to incorporate that into making a portfolio to show my works!

  • I often take inspiration from beach and city settings I have travelled to over the years, alongside movie and album cover art.

  • I tend to utilise a pastel colour pallette in my paintings, diluting the colours a lot with water. That way, I can easily add darker shades when I want more depth to appear.
